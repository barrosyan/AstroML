About AstroML

Space Missions

Space missions are exploratory journeys undertaken by various space agencies and organizations to study celestial objects, gather scientific data, and expand our understanding of the universe. These missions involve launching satellites, telescopes, and spacecraft to conduct observations, experiments, and research in outer space.

Astrophysics

Astrophysics is a branch of physics that focuses on studying the physical properties, behavior, and origins of celestial objects, such as stars, galaxies, and the universe as a whole. It employs various mathematical and observational techniques to unravel the mysteries of the cosmos.

Data Science

Data science is a multidisciplinary field that involves extracting insights and knowledge from large and complex datasets. It combines elements of statistics, mathematics, programming, and domain expertise to analyze and interpret data to make informed decisions and predictions.

Author: Yan Barros

Yan Barros is a Brazilian Data Scientist specialized in geospatial and astronomical data. He has created a rain prediction neural network model using real-time cloud images, satellite images, and temporal data. Yan has extensive experience in scraping data from various spatial and geospatial sources. He has also developed numerous data science projects and led a data team at Interjato Soluções.
